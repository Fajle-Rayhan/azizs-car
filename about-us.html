ABOUT US


We're "car guys", first and foremost. We're lucky enough to have turned our hobby into our profession. Heck, we're all over 40 and we're still playing with cars and trucks... what does that tell you?!?


We’re committed to providing you with the highest level of service of any of the Classic Vehicle Restoration shops out there so you’ll always choose us for your restoration, repair, and maintenance.


For most people, vehicle maintenance and repair is not fun or convenient. So, we want to make the process as easy, fun, and enjoyable as possible for you. We genuinely care about your vehicle and your experience – and it shows.


Our first-time customers continually become long-term clients time and time again, choosing us for all of their automotive needs.



We may not be related, but you’ll feel like we’re family.


We’re committed to auto repair and to serving you with quality and value. Our mechanics are highly skilled and have years of practical, hands-on experience.


Most of all: they love cars and care about yours.


Whether it’s our quick, quality, friendly service or the honesty and value we bring to every interaction, our customers love us.


Get to know our experienced Team. They’ve got the skills, the tools, and an overwhelming desire to help. Come see why no shop is as quick, qualified, or convenient as Fixed Auto Restoration.



The Crew


Our Team is made up of guys with over 20 years of experience.​


We're all Master Craftsmen at what we do – from body repair, metal fabrication, customization, paint, mechanical, and electrical.

​

We Love to Work on Cars


Our Team comes to work smiling and are happy to design your ride. They have a passion for Restoration on classic cars and are fully qualified to work on yours.



Leave it to Us


Doing it yourself doesn’t always pan out. Rely on our knowledge and experience instead. Your car or truck is in the best of hands.



We Truly Care


The right product extends the life of any car or truck. We bring advanced techniques and cutting-edge and latest technology to the table every time.



Our Promise to You


We promise to always provide value and outstanding service, regardless of repair size or difficulty.
