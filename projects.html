Our History


Having started out restoring 55-57 Thunderbirds, Marvin Hill grew his restoration business to the level where he needed a constant supply of 1955, 1956, and 1957 NOS and reproduction parts for his Thunderbirds. With the demand for parts being driven by the ongoing success of the restoration business, it was only a matter of time until Hill's Automotive took over as a parts distributor for 55-57 Thunderbirds. Hill's Thunderbird Center offers top of the line parts, including a complete line of reproduction, N.O.S., and used parts (as well as NOS Sheet Metal) for 1955, 1956, and 1957 Thunderbirds. Hill's is also offering the Resto-Mod Drive Train.

Quality, experience, and award winning are just a few of the words our customers associate with Hills Automotive. If you are looking for a classic car restoration, you have come to the right place. At Hill's Automotive, classic car restoration services is our specialty. We have been bringing classics back to life for over 35 years. Our experienced and award winning fabricators and body and paint departments are the reason why our cars stand out from the rest.
