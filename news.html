Honda Structural Body Repair Accreditation

Posted Monday, November 20, 2017 / by Neil Hodges

At Gaskell Motor Bodies we like to keep all our staff trained to the highest degree. We are pleased to announce that Tony our Senior Panel Technician has completed the Honda Structural Body Repair Accreditation.

As Gaskell Motor Bodies is a Honda approved Bodyshop we ensure your Honda is repaired to the highest quality finish and this is why we make sure our staff are Honda Structural Body Repair Accredited.

East Lancashire Hospice 10k

Posted Monday, December 4, 2017 / by Nick

Kickstart your 2018 fitness regime at the 5th annual East Lancashire Hospice 10k on Sunday 14th January

Entry is £12 for all runners.
On the day entries will be £15 and are subject to availability.

Held at Gaskell Motor Bodies, Gaskell Court, Alan Ramsbottom Way, Great Harwood, Lancs BB6 7UF. Registration will start at 9:30am on the day, with the race commencing at 11am.

Please read our conditions of entry before you register. If you have any questions please call 01254 287012. Your registration pack will be posted to you once we receive your details.

You can now register your child for the junior 2k race for just £3! This will start after the 10k has commenced. Please note that children will receive a medal for completing but not a t-shirt.
